Drawbacks of Goan sausages

Vir Sanghvi in His Book "Rude Food: The Collected Food Writings of Vir Sanghvi"
says that Indian cookery is not big in sausages.
There is, however, one part of India where they make great sausages(Which is Goa).
The spicy Goan sausages in a vinegary adaptation of Portuguese chorizo,and he loves them.
And then he writes "Sadly,nearly all the pork in Goa is unsafe-don't forget that Goans
use pigs before sanitation."

And so he always has to stop himself from ordering Goan sausages even though he is repeatedly
assured that now farm pigs in Goa and
that safety standards have improved.


NOTE:
This information is taken from the book "Food: The Collected Food Writings of Vir Sanghvi"(Page 313)
