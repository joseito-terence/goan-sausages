Origin of The Goan Sausages

This recipe was ealier used by the Portuguese sailors , to preserve the meat.

As it took days to reach their port from Goa and back again. And most of the food would get spoilt ,
so they used this method to preserve the meat(poke meat) on their long journey.

This then was later adopted by the Goans to preserve the meat during the monsoon seasons,as the fishermens would not go to the sea,
and so they would not get fish.

And also there were no refrigators to store the food during their time.
