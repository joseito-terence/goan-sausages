Goan Sausages

The most famous and most delicious sausages of Goa. In the konkani language known as Choris.
It is prepared with poke and several other ingredients.

It is usually boiled , fried, served in curry and also fried with potatoes.

Other sausage dishes are Sausage Pulao,Sausage Chilli Fry and the Sausage Bread.

Are widely sold in the markets of Goa.

Many people are involved in the manufacturing of the sausages

The Costa Factory is one factory which is involved in the manufacturing of the Goan Sausages
